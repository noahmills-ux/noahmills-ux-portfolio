Project Description

This was a solo project I took on to re-design the GiveSolar website. GiveSolar is a nonprofit social impact organization that aims to assist homeowners with low income in gaining access to solar energy through organizing funding sources and technical assistance for Habitat for Humanity partners. This website was designed on SquareSpace with custom coded HTML/CSS sections and menus. It was a really fun experience to work on a website for a non-profit and I am so excited to see how GiveSolar continues to grow and make a national impact!
