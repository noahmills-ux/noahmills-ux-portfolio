Project Description

This was a website re-design project I completed in the Fall of 2024. The website re-design was made on Shopify with one of the free themes. This project was a lot more simple than the other Shopify builds I have completed, but was a really fun experience to work on! I ended up purchasing my wife's engagement ring from Rocky's in the Fall of 2023, so having the chance to re-design their website really hit home for me. Really proud of how this one turned out and I am excited to see how Rocky's continues to grow in the future!
