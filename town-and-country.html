Project Description

This was a solo website re-design project I completed in the Fall of 2024. This was an immense project that involved lots of custom coding. Town & Country Furniture does not sell anything online, but they wanted to display their products and selectionsof furniture so that customers have a sense of what they can find in their store. This website re-design was built on Shopify and involved lots of custom coding to turn off all of the commerce features on the front end product pages. I really enjoyed working on this project and getting to know all of the ins and outs of Shopify.
