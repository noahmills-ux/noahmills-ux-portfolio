Choleta

A full Squarespace website rebuild for Chloeta, a government contractor with a wide and complex range of service offerings — designed and developed solo from start to finish. The previous site was severely outdated and needed a complete overhaul using the existing brand and color palette as the foundation. Over a six-month production timeline that included biweekly client meetings, every page was built independently. The most significant challenge was information architecture: mapping out the "What We Do" section in a way that was clear, intuitive, and scalable for a government contractor whose work spans multiple disciplines and service lines. Getting that structure right required multiple rounds of collaborative refinement. Beyond content strategy, the site features custom mega menus and a custom JavaScript- and CSS-driven resource library with internal page navigation built entirely from scratch. Chloeta is a large-scale site with significant room to grow as the organization continues to expand its content and media — and the architecture was designed from the start to support that growth without requiring a rebuild.
