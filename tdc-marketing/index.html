tdc marketing

A collaborative redesign of TDC's own company website that brought in significant creative input and direction from across the organization — but all of the advanced functionality was built from scratch by me. The most distinctive feature is a dynamic color-changing header system: in the mission statement block just below the hero on the homepage — and carried through key pages across the site — headline text cycles through the colors of TDC's logo, each corresponding to one of the company's color-coded service areas. This animation had never existed in this form before; it was built entirely from the ground up, and it's become one of the most identifiable visual moments on the site. More recently, the Story page was developed — a fully custom horizontal scroll-triggered timeline experience where vertical scrolling animates the page horizontally, walking visitors through the founding of TDC from Mike and Megan's early content-only roots to the full-stack marketing agency it is today. The project required navigating a shifting creative direction as the team worked through a major rebrand and office move simultaneously, with input and feedback coming from across the organization throughout production. What launched is a strong, evolving foundation — and one I'm proud to have helped build.
